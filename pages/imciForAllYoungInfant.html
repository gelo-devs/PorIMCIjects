IMCI PROCESS FOR
ALL YOUNG INFANTS (Birth up to 2 Months)

Has The Infant Just Been Delivered?

NO

  • Provide Emergency Newborn Care
  • Resuscitate using the Helping Babies Breathe (HBB) approach
  • Keep baby warm
  • ASSESS and CLASSIFY for POSSIBLE SERIOUS BACTERIAL INFECTION and provide any treatments
  • Support mother to initiate breastfeeding
  • Refer to maternity unit/hospital
